5 Alternative Travel Tips

01

Photograph Everything

IProof of the condition of your luggage when you dropped it off at the airport.It might not get you your money back, but it’s a start. And it certainly will stop hire companies charging you for damage they didn’t notice in the first place or help you to travel when the tickets have been lost.And what if you lose your phone?Make sure the photos are backed up to a secure cloud so you can access them from anywhere if needed.
